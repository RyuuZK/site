// 02 • MY JOURNEY

Building universes through code.

My relationship with development started early, at 16, when I wrote my first lines of code building websites and web interfaces. What began as curiosity quickly became a passion when I discovered the power of interactive storytelling through programming — developing complete Visual Novels using the Ren'Py engine.

Seeking to push the boundaries of interactivity and complex logic, I moved into three-dimensional environments — programming dynamic behaviors and ecosystems in Roblox Studio via Lua. That experimental, multidisciplinary background shaped my problem-solving mindset and brought me to Computer Engineering at FURG, where I am currently in my second year.

Today I balance the scientific rigor of engineering with hands-on development. I have built 2 original games applying software architecture, physics, and AI concepts — and I expand my academic reach through university research and extension projects, focusing on robust, high-performance technological solutions.
